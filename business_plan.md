## 4. RECOMMENDED AFFILIATE NETWORKS

### Tier 1: Start Here (Day 1)

#### TikTok Shop Affiliate (HIGHEST PRIORITY)
- **URL:** seller.tiktok.com → Affiliate tab
- **Why first:** Native to TikTok, no link-in-bio friction, in-app checkout = 3-5x higher CVR
- **Commission:** 5-25% depending on seller (you negotiate)
- **Payment:** Weekly, minimum $20
- **Approval:** Instant for accounts with 1,000+ followers, or apply with 0 followers via Seller plan
- **Best categories:** Beauty, gadgets, supplements, fashion

#### Amazon Associates
- **URL:** affiliate-program.amazon.com
- **Why include:** Highest consumer trust, massive product selection, great for "Amazon finds" content
- **Commission:** 1-10% by category (electronics 3%, beauty 10%, luxury beauty 10%)
- **Payment:** 60 days net, $10 minimum
- **Approval:** Instant, but requires 3 qualifying sales within 180 days
- **Pro tip:** Use Amazon's "List" feature to group products; promotes your entire store

#### ClickBank
- **URL:** clickbank.com
- **Why include:** Highest commissions (50-75%), digital products = instant delivery = higher CVR
- **Commission:** 50-75% of $27-$197 products = $15-$150/sale
- **Payment:** Weekly or biweekly, $10 minimum
- **Approval:** Instant — no approval needed per offer
- **Best categories:** Weight loss, relationships, make money online, health

### Tier 2: Add at Week 4+

#### ShareASale
- **URL:** shareasale.com
- **Why include:** 4,500+ merchants, many exclusive programs not on other networks
- **Commission:** Varies by merchant (8-30% typical)
- **Payment:** 20th of each month, $50 minimum
- **Approval:** Per-merchant approval required
- **Best merchants:** Reebok (7%), Etsy (4%), Wigs.com (15%), many supplement brands

#### Impact (impact.com)
- **URL:** impact.com
- **Why include:** Premium brand partnerships (Walmart, Lowe's, NordVPN, Semrush)
- **Commission:** Varies, typically $10-$150/sale for high-ticket partners
- **Payment:** Configurable by merchant
- **Approval:** Apply per-brand, takes 1-5 business days

#### CJ Affiliate (Commission Junction)
- **URL:** cj.com
- **Why include:** Home to major retail brands (Barnes & Noble, Overstock, Office Depot)
- **Commission:** 1-15% depending on retailer
- **Payment:** 20th of month, $50 minimum
- **Approval:** Per-advertiser approval

### Tier 3: Scale Phase (Week 8+)

#### Rakuten Advertising
- **URL:** rakutenadvertising.com
- **Why include:** Luxury and premium brands (Saks, Bergdorf Goodman, New Balance)
- **Best for:** High-ticket fashion, luxury goods, premium appliances

#### FlexOffers
- **URL:** flexoffers.com
- **Why include:** 12,000+ advertisers, competitive commissions, fast approval

## 5. LEGAL CHECKLIST

### FTC Disclosure Requirements (MANDATORY — do before first post)
- [ ] **#ad or #sponsored** must appear in caption for paid partnerships
- [ ] **#affiliate or #affiliatelink** must appear when earning commissions — this is REQUIRED by FTC
- [ ] Disclosure must be **above the fold** — not buried in hashtags
- [ ] In videos, verbally or visually disclose ("This video contains affiliate links")
- [ ] TikTok's built-in "Paid Partnership" toggle does NOT satisfy FTC requirements alone — add text disclosure too
- [ ] **Penalty for non-disclosure:** FTC fines up to $43,792 per violation
- [ ] Review FTC guidelines: ftc.gov/business-guidance/resources/disclosures-101-social-media-influencers

### Business Formation
- [ ] **Form LLC** in your state (recommended: Wyoming or Delaware for tax benefits)
  - Wyoming LLC: $100 formation + $60/year
  - Use: LegalZoom ($79+), ZenBusiness ($49+), or state website directly
- [ ] **Get EIN** (Employer ID Number) from IRS.gov — free, takes 5 minutes online
- [ ] **Open business bank account:** Mercury (free, online), Relay (free), or local credit union
- [ ] **Business credit card:** Ink Business Cash (5% on office/internet), or Chase Ink Unlimited (1.5% everything)

### Tax Obligations
- [ ] Track ALL income by affiliate network (you'll get 1099s for $600+ from each)
- [ ] Self-employment tax: Set aside 25-30% of net profit for taxes
- [ ] Deductible business expenses: Camera gear, software, internet, phone (business %), ad spend, home office
- [ ] Estimated quarterly taxes: Due Jan 15, April 15, June 15, Sept 15
- [ ] Hire a CPA once you hit $2,000/month — costs ~$1,500/year, saves 10x that

### Contracts & Agreements
- [ ] Read every affiliate network's Terms of Service before joining
- [ ] Common violations that get you banned: posting affiliate links on Pinterest without disclosure, cookie stuffing, fake reviews
- [ ] If recruiting sub-creators: have a written revenue share agreement (use HelloSign or DocuSign)
- [ ] Do NOT promote ClickBank health products with medical claims — FTC and FDA violations

### Platform-Specific Rules
- [ ] TikTok Community Guidelines: No paid promotion of weapons, tobacco, alcohol to minors
- [ ] Amazon Associates TOS: No posting affiliate links in emails, PDFs, or eBooks; no link shortening (use Amazon's own shortener)
- [ ] ClickBank: Cannot use PPC ads to bid on advertiser's branded keywords
- [ ] All networks: No cookie stuffing, no fake clicks, no incentivized traffic without disclosure
