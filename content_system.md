## 3. 10 PROVEN VIRAL VIDEO FRAMEWORKS

### Framework 1: "I tried X for 30 days"
**Structure (60-90 seconds):**
```
[0-3s]  HOOK: "I used [product] every single day for 30 days and here's what actually happened"
[3-10s] Problem setup: "I've struggled with [problem] for years"
[10-25s] Show the product, explain why you chose it
[25-50s] Week-by-week results with visual proof
[50-65s] Final reveal â€” be honest (builds trust = more conversions)
[65-75s] "Link in bio if you want to try it"
```
**Why it works:** Completion rate is high (people wait for the result). Perceived authenticity = trust = clicks.

**Hook variations:**
- "30 days, 1 product, ACTUAL results"
- "I gave this 30 days. Here's what nobody tells you."
- "Consistent use of X for 30 days â€” worth it?"

### Framework 2: "POV: You discovered X"
**Structure (15-30 seconds):**
```
[0-2s]  Text on screen: "POV: You just discovered the [product] that [benefit]"
[2-8s]  Show product in use, satisfying visual
[8-20s] Key feature demonstration
[20-25s] "It's in my bio"
```

**Why it works:** POV format creates immersive identification. Short = high completion rate = algorithm boost.

**Hook variations:**
- "POV: You found the skincare product that actually cleared your skin"
- "POV: You discovered why everyone is obsessed with this"

### Framework 3: "Things I wish I knew before buying X"
**Structure (45-75 seconds):**
```

[50-65s] Results + soft CTA: "I linked the one I use"
```
**Why it works:** Educational content gets saved (saves = massive algorithm signal). Saved videos get recommended to cold audiences.

### Framework 4: Unboxing + Honest Review
**Structure (60-90 seconds):**
```
[0-3s]  HOOK: "Okay I spent $[price] on this and..." (curiosity gap)
[3-15s] Unboxing â€” show packaging, first impressions
[15-35s] Testing it live on camera
[35-55s] Real reaction â€” pros AND cons (cons = credibility)
[55-70s] Final verdict + "Link in bio"
```
**Why it works:** Comments section floods with "link?" = free engagement boost. Cons make it credible = higher CTR than pure positive reviews.

**Critical:** Never fake your reaction. Viewers detect inauthenticity instantly.

**Structure (45-90 seconds, list format):**

**Optimization:** Put your highest-EPC product last (people remember the last thing most).

### Framework 6: Before/After Demonstrations
**Structure (30-60 seconds):**
```
[0-2s]  HOOK: Show the "before" â€” the problem state
```

### Framework 7: Duet/Stitch with Trending Content
**Structure (30-45 seconds):**
```
[0-3s]  React to original video's claim/question
[3-20s] Your response using your product as the answer
[20-35s] Demonstration
[35-45s] "Link in my bio" â€” soft close
```

**Why it works:** Piggybacks on existing viral momentum. TikTok algorithm actively promotes duets of popular videos.

**Strategy:** Find trending videos with 500K-5M views in your niche (not 50M+ â€” too competitive). Duet within 24-48 hours of their peak.

### Framework 9: "Stop Scrolling If You Need X"
**Structure (15-30 seconds, urgent format):**
```
[0-2s]  TEXT HOOK: "Stop scrolling if you've been looking for [specific problem solution]"
```

**Why it works:** Direct address creates pattern interrupt. Viewer self-selects = higher CTR from highly qualified traffic. Short = can be posted multiple times per day.

### Framework 10: Comparison Videos
**Structure (60-90 seconds):**
```
[0-3s]  HOOK: "I tested [Product A] vs [Product B] so you don't have to"
[3-20s] Test criteria (set expectations)

[75-85s] "Loser" gets a consolation: "Great if you're on a budget"
```
**Why it works:** High completion rate (viewers want the verdict). Comments war = engagement boost. Covers both products = captures wider audience.
